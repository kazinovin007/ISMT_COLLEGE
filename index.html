About ISMT College

Your Gateway to International Education

ISMT College (International School of Management and Technology) is dedicated to providing world-class education with a global perspective. We offer prestigious British degrees from Pearson BTEC and the University of Sunderland, ensuring our graduates are equipped with internationally recognized qualifications.

With a strong focus on practical skills and career readiness, ISMT prepares students for success in various professional fields. Our vibrant learning environment, coupled with experienced faculty and cutting-edge resources, makes ISMT the ideal place to achieve your academic and career aspirations.
